ABOUT ME

Currently, I am pursuing a Java Full Stack Development course at Jspiders, where I am gaining hands-on experience in Spring, Hibernate, Servlets, and front-end development. Additionally, I have earned certifications in Java, Python, and Design Thinking, which have strengthened my technical knowledge.
I am highly motivated to start my career in software development and am eager to contribute to a dynamic team. My goal is to apply my skills in developing scalable applications, solving real-world problems, and continuously learning new technologies.
